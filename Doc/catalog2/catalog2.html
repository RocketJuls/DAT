    Plants protecting tools

    Means of plant protection are divided into four main types: mechanical, chemical, biological and agrotechnical. However, the most rational thing is their simultaneous, correct combination. The most common method is chemical. There are thousands of different types of chemical protection preparations. But they should not be confused, let alone combined with chemical fertilizers. The methods of their influence on plants are completely opposite.

    It is necessary to know and distinguish chemical means of plant protection. After all, these drugs have a strictly defined object of influence (causing agent of disease, insect pests, weed species or family).

    Poisonous chemicals do not tolerate negligence. This is the type of drug that not enough just dilute in water and spray it on plants. If pesticides are used incorrectly, they can harm you, beneficial insects, harvest.
